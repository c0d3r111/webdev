About

Supreme Global Holdings Ltd.

Our Mission

Our mission has always been to delivervalue to our valuable customers by offering a rapidly changing assortment of high quality, uniquely-designed face masks, all atamazing values.

Registered in Hong Kong, with an operation aims to develop from a local scale to a more regional and global one, mainly serving our clientele and our partner companies around the world.

We started in 2009 as a relatively young company with a mission to help the world communities, in the food and supermarket industries.

  • Meet Market Demands

    We always strive for the best. Currently with the COVID-19 pandemic, we move to fulfil the high demands of PPEs and Super Safe face masks from countries, states and territories around the globe.

  • Develop Business Relationships

    We focus on our business lines with our strategic partners by developing closer relationship with them, and our associates.

Just as our mission has remained steadfast for decades, so has our commitment to acting as a responsible corporate citizen, and we bringthe philosophy to life through our corporate responsibility pillars.
